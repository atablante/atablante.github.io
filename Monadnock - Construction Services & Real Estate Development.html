COMPANY PROFILE

Monadnock was founded in Brooklyn over 40 years ago by Nick Lembo, and has always done business under that name.  Nick started the company as a masonry operation with just himself and three employees, but quickly expanded into a large high-rise union mason and eventually into General Contracting, Construction Management, and Real Estate Development.  Bringing in long time employee Peter Hansen as a partner in the late 1980’s and then Greg Bauso some years later, the company grew steadily to its current team of nearly 200 people with anywhere from 10-15 active job sites at any given time.

CONSTRUCTION SERVICES

Monadnock performs work as lump sum General Contractor or as either at-risk or as agent Construction Manager. Our affiliated company Monadnock Development is also able to provide partnership on any project in the form of fee developer or as equity participant.
